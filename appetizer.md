* 6 large eggs

* Ice

* 2 Tbsp. mayonnaise

* 1 1/2 Tbsp. sweet or dill pickle relish

* 1 tsp. yellow mustard

* 1/4 tsp. kosher salt

* 1/4 tsp. black pepper

* Garnish: paprika (optional)

1. boil eggs

2. make ice bath

3. chill eggs

4. peel eggs

5. halve eggs

6. make filling

7. fill egg
